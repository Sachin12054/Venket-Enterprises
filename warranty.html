Warranty Information

Comprehensive warranty coverage and support for all our products

Our Warranty Commitment

At Venkat Enterprises, we stand behind the quality of our products with comprehensive warranty coverage. We ensure that all equipment meets the highest standards of performance and reliability.

Warranty Coverage

Product Category Warranty Period Coverage
Survey Equipment (Auto Levels, Total Stations) 2-3 Years Manufacturing defects, calibration issues
Civil QC Lab Equipment 1-2 Years Mechanical failures, electrical components
Construction Tools 6 months - 1 Year Motor failures, manufacturing defects
Power Tools 6 months - 2 Years Motor, battery, and electronic components
Safety Equipment 6 months - 1 Year Material defects, manufacturing faults

What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects and material faults
  • Premature component failures under normal use
  • Calibration accuracy issues (for precision instruments)
  • Free repair or replacement of defective parts
  • Technical support and troubleshooting

What's Not Covered

  • Physical damage due to misuse, accidents, or negligence
  • Normal wear and tear of consumable components
  • Damage caused by improper installation or operation
  • Environmental damage (water, fire, extreme conditions)
  • Modifications or repairs by unauthorized personnel

How to Claim Warranty

Step 1: Contact Us

Reach out to our support team via phone, WhatsApp, or email with your product details and issue description.

Step 2: Provide Documentation

Share your purchase invoice, warranty card, and any relevant photos/videos of the issue.

Step 3: Assessment

Our technical team will assess the issue and determine the best course of action.

Step 4: Resolution

We'll either provide remote support, on-site service, or arrange for pickup and repair/replacement.

Service Support

  • Response Time: Within 24 hours for warranty claims
  • Service Network: PAN India service coverage
  • Pickup & Drop: Available for major equipment
  • Calibration: Annual calibration services available
  • Spare Parts: Genuine spare parts availability
